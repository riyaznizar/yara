ABOUT Yara

Sun kissed beaches, lush green mountains, timeless ruins and turquoise waters...This is what makes Sri Lanka the perfect place for the edifice of Yara's exquisite retreats. "Yara" Hotels & Resorts is a new, shining star that has emerged in the competitive sky of contemporary hospitality. With the essence of Yara's establishment being utmost customer satisfaction, our vision is to inspire versatility in the field of hospitality through affordability & sustainable living.

Our team thrives in knowing that the ability to relive and recreate memories lies in the threshold of liberality. Which is exactly why our structures are meticulously designed to capture the best of its surroundings while ensuring that our guests will remain luxuriously satisfied

WHY CHOOSE yara HOTEL?

We hope to take you on a story book adventure throughout our upcoming collection of hotels & resorts, set to open in some of the most pristine locations around the island with spectacular panoramic views.

No matter where you choose to stay with us, we guarantee to give you an authentic experience of our Island's renowned hospitality.

In Sri Lanka, Feel Welcome... At Yara, Feel Pampered.

Until we meet, we leave you with a traditional greeting from Sri Lanka for a long blessed life; Ayubowan!

who we are ?

Yara hotels & resorts is a subsidiary of 3N&Y - a well-established parent company that is self-financed, having invested in a variety of commercial sectors. With a team of experts assigned to head each organization independently, their investments range from Real estate to the field of Automobile trading, FMCG manufacturing and distribution, tea & spice exports, kitchen & bath ware solutions & leisure developments.

Headed by a group of intellectual entrepreneurs, 3N&Y has been actively & successfully operating for the past 2 decades, both in Sri Lanka and overseas. Taking into consideration the influx of tourists to Sri Lanka over the past few years, the company recently categorized into the hospitality industry and thus, YARA was born.
